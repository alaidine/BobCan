### The rules and lore

You are Bob and after you get out of work
there is an alien invasion. The alien start atacking you but you can only walk around at first. When you die you can resurect and you also get the possibility to earn new skills like jumping or shooting projectiles. The goal of the game is to get enough skill to beat the alien.
